There are many amazing channels you can find on Youtube if you want to learn some new Korean phrases and grammar or if you want to get to know Korean culture and lifestyle better. Here are some of my favorite Youtube channels, both in English and Polish.

Talk To Me In Korean


One of the best resources to learn Korean. On their Youtube channel you can find real-life conversations, stories, idoms and grammar explained and many many more.

Level: Beginner to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Talk to Me in Korean

Talk To Me In 100% Korean


Antother channel from Talk To Me In Korean, but this tima all content is in Korean.

Level: Intermediate to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Talk to Me in 100% Korean

Asian Boss


Great channel to get to know more about current cultural and political issues in Korea. You can listen to reporters interviewing Korean people on the streets of Seoul.

Level: Advanced (English subtitles provided)
Youtube channel link: Asian Boss

Pierogi z Kimchi (PL)


Daily vlogs and cooking recipes from a Polish woman living in Korea. Acompannied by her Korean husband and son, Pierogi z Kimchi provides fun and entertaining content that will help you get more familiar with Korean day-to-day life.

Youtube channel link: Pierogi z Kimchi

Pyra w Korei (PL)


Polish Youtuber living in Korea with her Korean husband and a dog named Sohee, introducing Korean clothing styles, interesting spots around Seoul and frightening true crime stories.

Youtube channel link: Pyra w Korei
