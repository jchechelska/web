There are many amazing channels you can find on Youtube if you want to hear some real-life Mandarin Chinese and get to know Chinese culture and lifestyle better. Here are some of my favorite Youtube channels, both in English and Polish.

Everyday Chinese


Great resource to review all the HSK words and grammar points as well as to hear some daily conversations.

Level: Beginner to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Everyday Chinese

Yoyo Chinese


Comprehensive online course with native Chinese dialogues.

Level: Beginner to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Yoyo Chinese

Chinese with Mandarin HQ


Short videos with street interviews, accent videos, practical phrases, pronunciation and many many more.

Level: Beginner to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Chinese with Mandarin HQ

每日中文课Free To Learn


Interesting channel with stories in Chinese.

Level: Intermediate to Advanced
Youtube channel link: Talk to Me in 100% Korean

Asian Boss


Great channel to get to know more about current cultural and political issues in China, Hong Kong and Taiwan. You can listen to reporters interviewing Chinese people on the streets of Baijin, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Taipei and many others.

Level: Advanced (English subtitles provided)
Youtube channel link: Asian Boss

Weronika Truszczyńska (PL)


Daily vlogs and and cultural insights from a Polish girl living and studying in Shanghai.

Youtube channel link: Weronika Truszczyńska
